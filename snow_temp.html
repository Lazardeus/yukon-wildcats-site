Weather & Safety Notice

Extreme Weather Policy

During severe weather conditions where it's unsafe for our crew to travel (including when we cannot safely exit our own driveway), service may be temporarily suspended for safety reasons.

Emergency Response

For immediate emergencies requiring urgent snow removal, we will assess conditions and respond as soon as it's safe to do so. Emergency surcharges may apply.

Service Resumption

All delayed services will be completed as soon as weather conditions improve and it's safe for our equipment and crew to operate.

Safety First: We prioritize the safety of our crew and customers above all else. Weather-related delays are beyond our control but we will communicate any changes promptly.
